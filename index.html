XAM is my simple drag & drop exam/question paper builder for schools, universities, and anyone else; plus it's free and open source!

What can XAM do?

Unique Save Structure
XAM saves to it's own .exam format to keep everything organised and easy to edit.
Export to PDF
When it's printing time, we export exams to PDF to retain their structure.
Two Sizes
We can render papers to either A4 or A5, with the same relative size of text, keeping it nice and readable.
Auto Sized Line Guides
What's annoying is when you print a paper from a .docx and all the ... line guides are messed up. XAM generates them to the specific length required when you export.

Why "XAM"?

Well, XAM is easy to say and to read, and was far less clunky that the long form - exam constructor
